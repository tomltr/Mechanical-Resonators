1849

1849 is a board game that encourages players to race against each other to a gold mine using jumps, lassos/whips, and robots to fend off against chasms, snakes, and quicksand. It utilizes platformer mechanics into a competitive dice-roll board game medium while presenting a steampunk twist on a classic point in American history.
